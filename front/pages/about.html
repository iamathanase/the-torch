About The Torch

Empowering communities through agricultural education and digital innovation across Uganda and South Sudan.

Our Mission

To empower refugee and internally displaced persons (IDP) communities in Uganda and South Sudan through comprehensive agricultural education, sustainable farming practices, and digital market access. We provide low-cost farming techniques, sustainable water use practices, and climate-appropriate crop selection to build long-term food independence and reduce reliance on external aid.

6-Month Initiative

Comprehensive training program running June-November 2026, targeting 40-60 refugee and IDP households with sustainable agricultural practices and digital literacy.

Digital Market Access

The Torch platform enables graduates to sell their produce directly to customers, generating sustainable income while supporting food security across the region.

Long-Term Impact

Building community ownership and train-the-trainer programs ensure lasting agricultural expertise and food independence for refugee and IDP communities.
